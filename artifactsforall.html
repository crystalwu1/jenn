Artifacts for All

Artifacts for All is an annual magazine that the editorial team of Medium Design Collective produces. This year our mission was highlighting designs that targeted the development of diversity and inclusivity in communities. I worked alongside the Editor in Chief as an Editor, Writer, and Creativity Curator to develop this 80+ page magazine.
