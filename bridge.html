Bridge X-ing

The Bridge X-ing project is an architectural design project where I designed both the aesthetics and efficiency of a structure. To elaborate on the idea of crossing a bridge, I designed the bridge so that the two trusses, which normally lie parallel to each other, intersect. Due to tight specifications and personal goals of achieving a structure that was as efficient as possible, this project required a long meticulous manufacturing process and careful weight distribution.
