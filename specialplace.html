My Special Place

Inspired by my childhood bunk bed and my twin little sisters, my Special Place is a 4-story architectural structure composed of ladders, skylights, glass ceilings, and more. The project was constructed out of environmentally conscious materials such as museum boards, used bamboo skewers, basswood, and water based glue.
