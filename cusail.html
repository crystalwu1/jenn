Motor Control

Due to the autonomous nature of the sailboat, the accuracy of the motor control is essential for precise navigation. By utilizing a belt system we found through past iterations the prevalence of skipping. To prevent this error, a belt tensioner is necessary. I spearheaded this design project with the guidance of the motor control team lead throughout the year.

LiDAR

The LiDAR is a remote sensing device that is used to ensure object detection and to increase maneuverability as needed. It is not waterproof, but because it is a visual sensor, it needs to be stationed at the top and front of the deck. I worked on this design project initially and guided the redesign and manufacturing process.
