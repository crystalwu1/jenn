Cowichan Cultural Centre

The Cowichan Cultural Centre aims to create a fundamental guideline for a Living Building Challenge Certified buildings and space. When working with the Cowichan Tribes, they indicated a need for a new exhibition space, grave house, storage space, meeting space, etc. We worked on creating a wide variety of design strategies to fulfill both the tribes visions and the Living Building Challenge Guidelines. I led the energy science research of the group; calculating low cost, low embodied carbon materials, high energy production methods and designing a net zero energy commercial building.

My Special Place

Inspired by my childhood bunk bed and my twin little sisters, my Special Place is a 4-story architectural structure composed of ladders, skylights, glass ceilings, and more. The project was constructed out of environmentally conscious materials such as museum boards, used bamboo skewers, basswood, and water based glue.
